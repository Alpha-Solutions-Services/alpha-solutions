10 Must-Have Features for E-commerce Sites

1) Fast storefront performance
2) Product filtering and smart search
3) Secure checkout with trusted payment options
4) Inventory sync and low-stock alerts
5) Mobile-first UX for product and checkout flows
6) Abandoned cart recovery

10) Fraud protection and account security controls

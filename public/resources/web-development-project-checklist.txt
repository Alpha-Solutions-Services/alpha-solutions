Web Development Project Checklist

1) Discovery and objectives
- Define business goals and measurable outcomes.
- Identify target audience and main conversion actions.
- Confirm timeline, budget, and key stakeholders.

2) Technical requirements
- Choose CMS/framework and hosting strategy.
- Define integrations (CRM, email, analytics, payments).
- Confirm performance, accessibility, and security requirements.

3) Content and design
- Finalize sitemap and page priorities.
- Prepare brand assets, copy, and media files.
- Review mobile-first layout and UX flow.

4) Launch readiness
- QA forms, links, redirects, and metadata.
- Validate Core Web Vitals and browser compatibility.
- Configure tracking, backups, and post-launch monitoring.

5) Post-launch
- Run first-week health checks and error monitoring.
- Review analytics and optimize conversion paths.
- Schedule ongoing maintenance and SEO updates.
